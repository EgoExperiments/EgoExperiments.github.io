SOLACE
Solace is a psychological thriller and survival horror game. Explore a world built on mystery and suspense. Experience a journey that will make you question reality. Uncover secrets that unfold in the next two games.
DEMO
The demo for Solace throws you right in the middle of a larger, more obscure story. You play as a discharged police officer. After the loss of his daughter, he's overwhelmed with insomnia fueled by his dedication to finding her. Being an ex-detective he uses his skillset to investigate missing persons cases in hopes that one day he might find whoever took his daughter. During the demo we follow the protagonist to the town of Lakeview to investigate a missing persons case, things quickly take a turn. His insomnia starts to skew his perception of reality yet again.
