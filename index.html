ABOUT US

At Ego Experiments Studios, we are driven by a singular vision: to create immersive experiences that challenge perceptions and inspire introspection. We believe that the most profound stories are those that make players look inward, confronting their deepest fears, desires, and internal conflicts.

By fusing psychological depth with innovative gameplay, we aim to redefine the boundaries of the horror genre, delivering experiences that not only terrify but leave a lasting impact, resonating with players long after the credits roll.

OUR GAMES

Here at Ego Experiments, we create games that push boundaries and immerse players in worlds where perception is constantly questioned. Each title invites players to explore rich, atmospheric environments filled with psychological tension and emotional complexity.

Solace

Psychological Horror

You play as a lifelong resident of Lakeview, a town where enforced conformity hides a sinister truth. Stripped of individuality, the people live in blind compliance, as the town’s eerie facade begins to crack. As your reality unravels, you must uncover the dark secrets lurking beneath the surface, where nothing is as it seems. Only in your mind can you find Solace...
